About Us

Smt. K.G. Mittal College was established in 1994 with the aim of providing quality education to students from all sections of society. The college is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra.

The college has excellent infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories, a spacious library, and modern classrooms. We focus on holistic development of students through academics, sports, and cultural activities.

Our faculty members are highly qualified and experienced, dedicated to nurturing the potential of every student. The college provides a conducive environment for learning and personal growth.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in their careers and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in higher education that nurtures innovation, research, and holistic development of students.

The college offers undergraduate programs in Science, Commerce, and Management streams. We have excellent placement records with students placed in reputed companies.
