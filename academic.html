Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc IT)

3-year undergraduate program focusing on information technology concepts, software development, and system administration.

Program Objectives

  • To provide strong foundation in IT concepts
  • To develop programming and problem-solving skills
  • To prepare students for IT industry careers

Curriculum

Semester I

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Computer Organization
  • Digital Electronics

Semester II

  • Data Structures
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc CS)

3-year undergraduate program with focus on computer science theory, algorithms, and system design.

Program Objectives

  • To provide strong foundation in CS fundamentals
  • To develop algorithmic thinking
  • To prepare students for software development careers

Curriculum

Semester I

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Calculus
  • Digital Electronics
  • Principles of Management

Semester II

  • Data Structures
  • Computer Organization
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Probability and Statistics

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

3-year undergraduate program in commerce affiliated to University of Mumbai.

Specializations

  • Accountancy & Finance (BAF)
  • Business Economics
  • Banking & Insurance
